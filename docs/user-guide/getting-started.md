# Getting Started

## What is NexTraceOne?

NexTraceOne is your organization's unified platform for:

- **Service Governance** — Catalog, ownership, and lifecycle management for all services
- **Contract Governance** — REST, SOAP, Kafka, and background service contract management
- **Change Confidence** — Validate and track production changes with blast radius analysis
- **Operational Reliability** — Incident management, runbooks, and team reliability tracking
- **AI-Assisted Operations** — Governed AI assistant, analysis, and IDE integrations
- **Source of Truth** — Single authoritative view of services, contracts, and operational knowledge

## Logging In

1. Navigate to the NexTraceOne URL provided by your organization.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. If MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) is enabled, complete the verification step.
4. After authentication, select your **tenant** if you belong to multiple organizations.

> **Tip:** If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page to receive a reset link.

## First Steps After Login

### Dashboard

After login, you arrive at the **Dashboard** — your personalized overview showing:

- Key metrics relevant to your role
- Recent activity across services and contracts
- Quick actions for common workflows

### Navigation

The **sidebar** on the left provides access to all modules:

| Section | What You'll Find |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Services** | Service catalog, service details, dependency graph |
| **Contracts** | Contract catalog, studio, governance, workspace |
| **Changes** | Change catalog, releases, promotion workflows |
| **Operations** | Incidents, runbooks, reliability, automation |
| **AI** | AI Assistant, model registry, policies, audit |
| **Governance** | Compliance, FinOps, teams, domains, reports |
| **Integrations** | Connector hub, ingestion monitoring |

### Global Search

Use the **Search** feature (available from the top navigation) to find services, contracts, runbooks, and documentation across the entire platform.

## Key Concepts

### Tenants
A **tenant** represents your organization or business unit. All data in NexTraceOne is isolated per tenant.

### Environments
Environments (Development, Staging, Production) allow you to track services and changes across deployment stages.

### Services
A **service** is any deployable unit — REST API, SOAP service, Kafka producer/consumer, or background service. Each service has an owner, contracts, and operational metadata.

### Contracts
A **contract** defines the interface of a service — its endpoints, schemas, and behavior. Contracts follow a lifecycle: Draft → In Review → Approved → Locked → Deprecated → Retired.

### Teams
**Teams** own services and are responsible for their reliability and governance compliance.
