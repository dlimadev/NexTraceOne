# Governance & Reports

## Compliance

Navigate to **Governance** → **Compliance** to monitor organizational compliance:
- Overall compliance score across governance packs
- Compliant vs. non-compliant service breakdown
- Remediation tracking for non-compliant items

## FinOps

NexTraceOne provides contextualized FinOps views at multiple levels:

### Executive FinOps
High-level cost overview with trend analysis, efficiency ratings, and optimization opportunities.

### Domain FinOps
Cost breakdown by business domain with waste signals and optimization recommendations.

### Team FinOps
Per-team cost tracking with efficiency comparisons and trend analysis.

### Service FinOps
Individual service cost attribution with reliability correlation.

> **Key principle:** NexTraceOne FinOps is always contextualized by service, team, and domain — not just raw cloud costs.

## Risk Management

### Risk Center
Central view of all risk indicators across the organization with severity-based filtering and trend tracking.

### Risk Heatmap
Visual representation of risk distribution by category, domain, and team. Risk levels: Critical, High, Medium, Low.

## Maturity Scorecards

Track organizational maturity across governance dimensions:
- Ownership coverage
- Contract coverage
- Documentation completeness
- Operational readiness

Scores are shown per team and domain with color-coded progress indicators.

## Executive Overview

The **Executive Overview** provides C-level dashboards with:
- Platform health summary
- Top risks and action items
- Maturity trends
- Cost optimization status

## Enterprise Controls

Monitor and enforce enterprise-wide governance controls:
- Contract governance compliance
- Source of Truth completeness
- Change governance adherence
- Security posture

## Reports

Generate and view governance reports segmented by persona:
- Executive summaries
- Technical deep-dives
- Compliance attestations
- Trend analysis
